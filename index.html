About EaaP Marketplace

We turn skills into bookable moments

EaaP Marketplace transforms local skills, passions and moments into bookable experiences. Hosts list time-bound, location-aware experiences (workshops, tours, mentorship sessions, events) and guests book them like products — simple, secure and social.

Powered by smart booking, AI recommendations and trust-first profiles, EaaP makes it effortless to find experiences that match your vibe, budget and schedule. Whether you're exploring a new hobby or booking a private mentorship, EaaP connects you with authentic hosts.
